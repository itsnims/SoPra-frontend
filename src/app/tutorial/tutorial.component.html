Expedition Cards

0) In 2 player mode select the playing piece you want to move

1) Choose the expedition card you want to play

2) Choose the tile you want to move to

3) Press "Move"


Buy from Market

1) Choose hand cards with sufficient value

2) Choose a buyable market card

3) Press "Buy"


Remove Blockade

1) Select a strong enough card

2) Press the white number next to the blockade

3) Press "Move"

Action Cards

- Transmitter

1) Choose the Transmitter Card

2) Choose the Card you want from the Market

3) Press "Use action card"


- Native

1) Choose the native

2) Choose the tile you want to move to

3) Press "Move"


- Others

1) Choose the action card you want to play

2) Press "Use action card"

3) Trash a card if possible and desired
